Opens like a real app, no browser bars, straight to the action.

📱 On iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button ⬆️ at the bottom.
  3. Scroll and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. Tap Add — the BNB icon appears on your home screen.
  5. Open it and tap Allow to get updates. 🎉

🤖 On Android (Chrome)

  1. Open this site in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Tap “Add to Home screen” / “Install app.”
  4. Confirm — the BNB icon appears on your home screen.
  5. Open it and allow notifications to get updates. 🎉

Already installed? Open the BNB icon from your home screen instead of your browser to get updates.
